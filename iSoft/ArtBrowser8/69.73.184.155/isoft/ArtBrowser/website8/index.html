How the App Will Work

ArtBrowser app is a project that combines digital and creative enterprises. It is a new platform for emerging and established artists to sell and share their work online. Here, our partners will be able to create a profile, telling buyers and art lovers about their work and practice. The app is for those interested in purchasing or browsing art.

One image will be shown at a time, with a key focus on the aesthetics, rather than statement or prior success of the artist. It also allows for art lovers to experience a new way of buying and finding art, which is open to the masses and free from criticism of taste or style. ArtBrowser will change the way we look and sell art.

By using a purely visual approach, art lovers will have the opportunity to develop their own taste. We also believe that the change of setting from looking at art by bringing the gallery to your fingertips.'
